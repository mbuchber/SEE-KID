Examples

    

This section contains three different examples which were modeled with SEE++:

 

1.Abducens palsy - as an example of an unilateral muscle palsy of a straight eye muscle
2.Superior oblique palsy - as an example of a palsy of an oblique muscle
3. Supranuclear gaze palsy - as a demonstration of a gaze palsy due to innervational causes

 

These examples offer an introduction to the system and the reader should be able to understand them without knowing the program in detail.

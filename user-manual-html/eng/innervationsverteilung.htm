Distribution of Innervations

    

The distribution of innervations dialog controls the muscles activation potential based on the stimulations created by the motor nuclei of the cranial nerves. This distribution is directly mapped to the innervations of each eye and therefore does not represent the real anatomical structure unambiguously. Rather different nuclear interferences can be modeled through adequate adaptations of the distribution of innervations for the left and/or the right eye.

 

The distribution of innervations exists for all eyes that can be simulated with SEE++ (left eye, right eye and reference eye). This explanation uses the left eye exemplarily. You can access this dialog via the main menu under "Data->Left Eye->Distribution of Innervations" or via the Treeview under "Medical Data->Left Eye->Distribution of Innervations".

 

 

For a detailed explanation of the effects caused by changes see the chapter force model (motor nuclei).

 

All values provided in this dialog are specified in percentage.
